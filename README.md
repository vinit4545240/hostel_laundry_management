# 🧺 Hostel Laundry Management System

A smart and efficient web-based solution designed to simplify hostel laundry operations. The Hostel Laundry Management System enables students to conveniently schedule laundry services, monitor the status of their clothes in real time, and receive timely updates—all through a seamless digital platform.

By eliminating manual records, reducing scheduling conflicts, and improving communication between students and laundry staff, the system enhances the overall hostel experience while ensuring a smooth and organized laundry workflow.

## ✨ Features

* 🔐 Secure User Authentication
* 📅 Laundry Slot Booking & Scheduling
* 👕 Real-Time Laundry Status Tracking
* 🔔 Automated Notifications & Reminders
* 📊 Admin Dashboard for Service Management
* 📱 Responsive and User-Friendly Interface

## 🎯 Objective

To modernize hostel laundry services through automation, providing a convenient, transparent, and time-saving solution for students and administrators alike.

**Transforming traditional hostel laundry management into a smart, hassle-free digital experience.**
